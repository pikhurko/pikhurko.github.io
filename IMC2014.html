    This is an informal problem-solving seminar for Warwick's undergraduates. One of objectives would be to select and prepare Warwick's team for the International Mathematical Competition that takes place annually around the end of July. Those students who would like to be considered for the team should attend all classes and do all tests and homework assignments.

    No prior registration is required and you do not need to be a math major in order to attend (MMath and MSc students are also eligible).

    Further information will be added here in due time. As an extra preparation for the seminar, we recommend solving past problems (available online with solutions) from the following competitions:

  • International Mathematical Competition (IMC)
  • Putnam Competition
  • Vojtech Jarnik Mathematical Competition

    • Good luck!
