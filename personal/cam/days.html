My First Few Days in Cambridge

My first few days 
Have lovely gone 
I've got much friends 
We all have fun.
My first few days 
Are like a wine: 
They make you drunk - 
You are just fine. 
My first few days 
A flow of joy, 
Whatever they bring 
I do enjoy.
My first few days 
They go along 
Being so short - 
Remembered long. 
The verse written October 1995
