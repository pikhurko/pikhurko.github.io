Hiking Trip on 5 July (Sunday)

There will be an informal hiking trip on 5 July to which the participants of the LMS-CMI Research School and the 25th British Combinatorial Conference who happen to be around are cordially invited. We meet 1:30pm at the front entrance to the Mathematics Institute (building 38 on the campus map). Then we will walk to the Abbey Fields in Kenilworth (6-7km walk, large part going through a nice countryside).

There are various things that people may self-organise to do once we are there, such as

  • listen to a band playing (2:30-4:30pm);
  • visit Kenilworth Castle (which is open until 6pm);
  • picnic (there are about 4-5 picnic tables and plenty of grass; one can get food from Sainsburys and Waitrose which are very close);
  • do some sports: eg there are a swimming pool and (free, on first-come basis) tennis courts;
  • continue the hike making 15-20km loop ending back on the campus (if there is interest, we will lead it or provide a map).
Other options of getting back to the campus include:
  • walking back the same way (a very clear and well marked route);
  • calling a taxi (eg Kenilwoth-based Call-Line Taxis at 01926267068 or campus-based Trinity Street Taxis at 02476999999);
  • taking bus 11 or U17 (rather infrequent on Sundays).
Anybody interested (including friends/partners) is welcome to join. This trip is not sponsored by the School/Conference so its participants will need to pay all their expenses.

We hope to see many of you there!
